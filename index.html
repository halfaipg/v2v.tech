About v2v.tech

v2v.tech is a specialized IT consulting firm focusing on virtualization migration services. With years of experience in enterprise environments, our team provides expert migration solutions that address the real challenges organizations face when dealing with expensive renewals and vendor lock-in.

We understand that migration projects can be complex and risky, especially when driven by cost pressures and tight timelines. That's why we've developed proven methodologies and tools to make your transition between virtualization platforms, physical infrastructure, and cloud environments as smooth as possible.

Our in-house development team creates fully custom solutions and tools tailored to your specific migration needs. Whether it's automating complex workflows, building specialized conversion tools, or developing custom migration scripts, we ensure each solution perfectly fits your unique infrastructure requirements and business constraints.

We work with organizations of all sizes - from SMBs looking for cost-effective alternatives to enterprises facing complex multi-site migrations. Our expertise spans Windows-heavy environments, Linux workloads, and hybrid cloud scenarios, ensuring we can handle any migration challenge you face.
